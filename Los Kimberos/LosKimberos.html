Born in the vibrant heart of San Francisco's Mission District in 2015, Los Kimberos has been setting dance floors on fire with authentic salsa that'll make you weak in the knees!

A LEGACY
OF PASSION

Los Kimberos was founded by the visionary Anthony Price, whose passion for authentic salsa and dedication to Latin music created the foundation of what we are today. Anthony's spirit, energy, and love for the craft continue to inspire every note we play.

Following Anthony's passing, his legacy lives on through Samuel Villazana Price, son of Toni, who stepped up to carry the torch forward. Under Samuel's direction, Los Kimberos continues to honor the band's roots while pushing boundaries and bringing fresh energy to the Bay Area salsa scene.

"We play not just for ourselves, but in honor of those who paved the way. Anthony's vision lives on in every performance."

— Samuel Villazana Price
